this is qwer hacks.

a place where, given a total of 12 - 24 thrilling hours with your team, your idea, and our fuel (in the form of snacks, drinks, rainbows, and more), you produce a fantastic project, while forming lasting relationships and chugging more caffeine and sparkly drinks than you ever thought possible.

FAQ

tell me more!
qwer hacks is ucla's premier intercollegiate hackathon: for 12 - 24 hours, nearly 250 awesome student hackers will self-identify. oh, and create amazing products meant to help underrepresented groups.
who can come?
any intersectional individual 16 or above enrolled in an academic institution from anywhere in the world. we admit high school students on a case-by-case basis for the day, and also welcome beginners - qwer hacks is meant to be a learning experience! we want you to come here to have fun and leave having learned something.
how do teams work?
you can sign up as a team of up to four members. make sure that all of your team members submit an application! if you don't have a team before the event, don't fret! we will help you find fellow hackers to work with once you arrive.
what should i bring?
a student ID, a laptop, and a charger are the only necessities. we recommend you bring clothes, toiletries, and a sleeping bag as well!
what can I create?
absolutely anything you want - websites, apps, hardware, you name it. we encourage you to create a product that positively impacts the lgbtqia+ community, but welcome any product that aims to help underrepresented groups.
how much will it cost?
it's absolutely free! we'll be providing you with meals, a place to hack, and most likely rainbow-themed swag. we may also be offering travel reimbursements on a case-by-case basis!
how will i get there?
ride the sweet, sweet rainbow of pride and excitement. more details tbd.
anything else i should know?
there are a few things we don't encourage; you should refer to the MLH Code of Conduct here for some guidelines to follow. also, don't be a BUTT.
sounds great, how do I apply?
applications will release soon, keep an eye out and follow our social media to stay hyped and in the loop!
help!
shoot us an email at qwerhacks@gmail.com and we'll get back to you before you can say "qwer hacks is awe-!"
