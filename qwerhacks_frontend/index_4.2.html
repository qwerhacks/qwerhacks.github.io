When: 01.25-26.2020 [Winter Quarter]
Where: Ackerman Grand Ballroom, UCLA

Applications will open late November — follow us on social media for updates!

QWER Hacks is UCLA’s first LGBTQIA hackathon! For 24 hours, around 250 amazing student hackers will work together to create fantastic and innovative products meant to help underrepresented groups.

Through empowering talks from queer individuals in tech, technical and diversity & inclusion workshops, and the development of new products, QWER Hacks aims to increase the visibility of and celebrate the queer community in STEM, as well as engage and bring together queer individuals and allies.

FAQ

WHO CAN COME?

Any student who’s of at least 18 years of age and enrolled in a postsecondary academic institution somewhere in the world is invited to participate! We welcome beginners :) QWER Hacks is meant to be a learning experience! We want you to come here to have fun and leave having learned something valuable. We also hope you discover a community within tech at QWER Hacks.

HOW DO TEAMS WORK?

You can sign up as a team of up to four members. But make sure that all of your team members submit an application! if you don’t have a team before the event, don’t fret! We will help you find fellow hackers to work with once you arrive.

WHAT SHOULD I BRING?

Your student ID, a laptop, and a charger are the only necessities. We recommend you bring clothes, toiletries, and a sleeping bag as well!

WHAT CAN I CREATE?

Absolutely anything you want - websites, apps, hardware, you name it! We encourage you to create a product that positively impacts the LGBTQIA community or other underrepresented groups in tech.

HOW MUCH WILL IT COST?

It’s absolutely free! We’ll be providing you with meals, a place to hack, and company and rainbow-themed swag.

ANYTHING ELSE I SHOULD KNOW?

There are a few things we do not encourage; please refer to the MLH code of conduct here (link).

SOUNDS GREAT, HOW DO I APPLY?

Applications will open mid-December! Follow us on Facebook and keep an eye out for upcoming announcements!

I STILL NEED HELP!

Shoot us an email at qwerhacks@gmail.com and we’ll get back to you before you can say “QWER Hacks is awe-!”
